Game Concept

Title: Empires Fall
Game Type: Base Defender
Target Audience: Research
Platforms: Browser

Game Overview

Empires Fall is a cooperative strategy game where players must defend their village from a series of world enemies that threaten the village. Players can choose to build up defenses, gather resources, or engage the enemies directly in skirmishes along its path to the village. The game features a blend of melee and ranged combat, alongside a building system for creating structures to aid in defense.

Core Gameplay Mechanics

Combat: Players engage in melee and ranged combat against the enemies and their minions. Also allowing players to build offensive structures to enhance their offensive capabilities, or alternatively build defensive structures to delay the enemies, giving players more time.
Building: Utilizing blueprints, players place structures with various capabilities, including; but not limited to: offensive, defensive and utility. These structures can be freely placed, rotated, and have their own health points, these buildings can then also be repaired by players.
Resource Management: Players gather resources to build structures and upgrade their capabilities, resources range between standard resources which are abundantly available as well as special resources spawned from defeated enemies.

Core Game Systems

Persistence: Player progress and server dynamics persist online, with resources and player levels influencing the game world.
Enemy Progression: An enemy will spawn somewhere in the world and will destroy every building in it’s path to get to the heart of the village. Every time the enemy is stopped a count will go up. This count is a streak, that will give the players rewards how higher it becomes. When the enemy isn’t stopped in time and breaks the heart of the village, all enemies are reset and the streak will be set to 0 again and the players can start over again.
Proximity: Agents active in the game have something called Proximity. This system tracks all agents within range and also what kind of agent they are. This versatile system can be used to detect agents within the attack range of the enemy or offensive structures, it can be used to detect other players to boost XP when performing actions together, and much more!

Monetisation (Optional)

Description: Monetisation strategies could include cosmetic items for both characters and structures, along with possibly offering premium blueprints or emote packs. These options provide personalization without affecting gameplay balance.
Cosmetic Items: Skins for characters and buildings.
Premium Blueprints: Advanced building designs that do not offer combat advantages.
Emote Packs: Additional communication options for players.

Narrative

The core of Empires Fall revolves around a community coming together to fend off a colossal threat. The narrative can explore themes of unity, resilience, and the balance between defense and resource management in the face of an overwhelming enemy.

Conclusion

Empires Fall promises a unique blend of strategy, building, and cooperative gameplay, set against the backdrop of defending a village from a formidable world enemies. Its core mechanics focus on teamwork, strategy, and resource management, offering players a dynamic and engaging experience that evolves with the community's efforts. With a simple yet deep building system and a focus on non-verbal communication, the game is designed to be accessible yet challenging, inviting players from around the globe to join forces and protect their village.
