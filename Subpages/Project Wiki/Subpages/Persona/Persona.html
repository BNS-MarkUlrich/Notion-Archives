Why do people play browser games?People play web browser games for various reasons, which we can divide into general motives andspecific benefits of web browser games:General motives for playing games:• Fun and relaxation: Games provide a way to escape from the daily routine and have fun.• Challenge and achievement: Players experience satisfaction by overcoming obstacles andimproving their skills.• Social interaction: Games can provide a platform to socialize and collaborate with friends or otherplayers.• Stories and fantasy: Games transport players to fictional worlds and let them experience uniqueadventures.• Discovery and learning: Players can learn new things and apply their knowledge in an interactiveenvironment.Specific benefits of web browser games:• Easy accessibility: Web browser games are playable on virtually any device with an internetconnection, without installation or downloads.• Free or low barrier to entry: Many web browser games are free to play or require only a lowthreshold to start.• Time-flexible: Web browser games can be played in short bursts or longer sessions, depending onavailable time.• Wide range of genres: There is a wide variety of web browser games available, from casual puzzlesto complex strategies, catering to different preferences.• Low system requirements: Web browser games typically require less powerful hardware thanother games, making them accessible to a broader audience.• Educational: Some web browser games are designed to be educational, teaching players aboutvarious subjects such as science, history, or language.• Problem-solving skills: Many web browser games require players to think creatively and solveproblems to win.• Cognitive skills: Playing web browser games can improve hand-eye coordination, reflexes, andreaction time.• Easy accessibility: Web browser games are often free to play and require no installation, makingthem easy to try.
