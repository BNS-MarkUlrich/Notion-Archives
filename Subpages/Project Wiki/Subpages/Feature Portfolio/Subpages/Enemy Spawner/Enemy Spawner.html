Description

This page describes and showcases the Enemy Spawner feature. Which spawns enemy after x amount of time and gives them a path to follow.
ALT

Functional Design

We decided that the after an x amount of times a random enemy would spawn at a random path, so that the player don’t know what they are up against and where the enemy will be.
The spawning cycle would look like this:
System gets a random enemy and a random waypoints path
Enemy gets spawned and a countdown starts
After the enemy is spawned the players can kill it or it will destroy the heart of the village
When the countdown hits zero, everything form step 1 gets repeated
We chose this flow, because we wanted to the players to feel a bit of pressure that you don’t know where and what is coming to them. This helps the players to communicate of what is coming for them.

Technical Design

For this feature we need 3 things:
Waypoint Paths: The enemy needs a path to the heart of village. A path contains waypoints that will lead the enemy to the heart. These waypoints can be hand made in the hierachy of the chosen scene as empty gameobjects. Then the waypoints will be saved in a spawnpoint script, that will give the first waypoint as spawn position and the rest of the waypoints to the enemy when it spawns.
After the enemy has spawned in, it gets a random waypoint path, sets the spawnposition to the enemy and then gives the enemy it’s path. Then the enemy will walk down that path thanks to Pathfinding & Movement.
Spawning: For spawning enemies we need to have a spawning function. This function will randomly pick a enemy prefab from a list and then spawn it in or create it with the help of the poolsystem. After that it wil get an random waypoint path, it gets health and the healthbar is activated for every current client. Then the enemy will start to walk to the heart of the village and for this feature we will not do anything with the enemy anymore, but it does start a countdown. When the countdown reaches 0, it will spawn a new enemy and repeats the cycle.
Timer: After an enemy has spawned a countdown will takes place, this will be a simple coroutine that waits until x amount of time, before calling the spawning function again. The time how long it will be taking can be adjust in the inspector.
