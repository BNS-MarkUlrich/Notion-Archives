Description

This document details the design choices for the player re-spawning feature in our game. It outlines the mechanics triggered when a player's health drops below zero, including the appearance of a "Death Screen," options for timed or instant re-spawn, and the ability for other players to revive them. This feature aims to maintain gameplay fluidity and enhance player interaction by allowing quick and strategic returns to the game.

Functional Design

The moment that the health of the player gets below 0, the player needs to enter a “Death state”. When the player is in this state the next things will happen:
Death Screen:
When the player “dies”, a screen should appear that indicates that the player has died.
When a player dies, they will re-spawn on the position where they died after x amount of seconds and other players can speed up this process. Players will also get the option to directly re-spawn in the heart of the village, skipping the re-spawn timer. This allows players who do not wish to wait in spot for a re-spawn to instantly re-spawn and start playing again, at the expense of having to walk back to where they were if they so wish to return there.
There will also be a button that allows the player to re-spawn immediately around the centre of the village.
Revive-able:
When the player has “died” this player gains the ability t be revived by other players. The player that “died” becomes interact-able to others, when interacted with the dead player plays its revive animation and immediately re-spawns.

Technical Design

Revivable:

At Start, the Revivable script adds the OnDie and OnResurrectede events from the HealthData
to the corresponding functions of OnDie() and OnRevive().
Listener Functions:
When The health of the player reaches below 0, The function OnDie() will be activated. In here the players interactions and functionalities will be deactivated and a “Death Screen” will activate.
When The player gets Revived, The function OnRevive will be activated. In here the processes of the OnDie() function will be reverted.
Force Re-spawn:
When this function is called it takes the “_reSpawnLocation” and sets the position of the player to this vector on the client and server.
After this the Healthdata function Resurrect() is called and the player “Alive” state is set to true.
Alive State:
When one of the listener functions is activated the “Alive” state of the player will be changed to the corresponding state.
Interaction: If the players “Alive” state is set to true, This lets other players interact with the current “Dead” player. When interacted with when “Dead”, The OnRevive function will be activated and revive the player.

RespawnTimer:

The moment that the script is activated a co-routine countdown is activated. In this the given count down amount is taken and set as the starting number. The moment the script is deactivated, the co-routine timer is deactivated and resets when reactivated.
After the countdown reaches 0, The reference to the players “Healthdata” is called to the function "Resurrect" to revive the player
