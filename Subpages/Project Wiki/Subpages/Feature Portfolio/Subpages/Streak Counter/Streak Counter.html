Description

The Streak Counter is a feature designed to enhance player progression in the game. It provides a count of bosses the players have defeated in a session, thus promoting a sense of achievement. Additionally, it serves as an indicator to new players about the duration of the session. The counter is technically simple, it increments based on a 'die' event of a boss, which is triggered every time a boss is defeated. This system integrates with the existing enemy spawner that produces a boss after fixed intervals, providing a seamless tracking of player progress.

Functional Design

Our primary aim was to give the player a good sense of progression as they engage in defeating bosses and protecting the village heart. We wanted to create an element of the game that would reflect the player's accomplishments, thus enhancing their gaming experience. As such, we decided to introduce the concept of a 'streak'. The streak is essentially a counter that displays the total number of bosses a player has defeated in a single session.
Not only does it provide a sense of achievement for the player, but it also acts as a helpful indicator for new players who join the session. By looking at the streak, new players can quickly gauge the duration of the ongoing session and understand the level of challenge they might be stepping into.

Technical Design

In terms of technical design, the streak counter is elegantly simple yet effective. It operates by keeping track of each time a boss is defeated, and incrementing the counter by one on each occasion. To facilitate this, we leveraged our existing enemy spawner system which already had the capability to spawn a boss after a specific amount of time.
The spawner system also maintains a record of the currently active bosses. Therefore, when a new boss is spawned, we added a listener to the 'die' event associated with it. This event is triggered whenever a boss is defeated, notifying the streak counter to increment its count. The integration of the streak counter with the existing spawner system ensures seamless and accurate tracking of player progress.
